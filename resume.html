Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Sun Yat-Sen University, School of Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in Embodied Intelligence with a focus on Reinforcement Learning and Generative Models for Robotics Control. Previously, I worked as a Hardware Engineer at Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. and during my Master's degree at Sun Yat-Sen University (State Key Laboratory of Optoelectronic Materials and Technologies), I was mainly dedicated to the application of artificial intelligence in 3D display and VR/AR technologies.

My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics, where I explore how generative modeling and reinforcement learning can be unified to create embodied agents capable of reasoning, adaptation, and real-time decision-making in the physical world.

Research Interests
Embodied Intelligence - Developing intelligent agents capable of perceiving, reasoning, and acting in physical environments
Reinforcement Learning - Advanced algorithms for decision-making and control in robotics and autonomous systems
Generative Models for Robotics - Applying generative models for robotics control and motion planning

个人简介

我目前是中山大学计算机科学与工程学院的博士研究生，研究方向为具身智能，专注于强化学习与机器人控制的生成模型。此前，我曾在华为技术有限公司担任硬件工程师。在中山大学攻读硕士学位期间（国家光电材料与技术重点实验室），我主要从事人工智能在3D显示和VR/AR技术中的应用研究。

我的研究处于人工智能与机器人技术的交叉领域，探索如何将生成模型与强化学习相结合，创造能够在物理世界中进行推理、适应和实时决策的具身智能体。
